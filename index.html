About Us

Manufacturer of Machine Parts & Tooling

Tathastu Enterprises is a specialized manufacturer of machine parts and tooling used in tyre manufacturing industries. Based in Thane West, Maharashtra, we serve some of India's largest tyre producers with precision-engineered components.

We specialize in manufacturing Tyre Building Drums, Clamping Rings, BPR, Bead Rings, PCI Rings, Solid Rims, and Mould Spacers. In addition, we perform all types of fabricated works and precision machining to meet the demanding requirements of tyre manufacturers.

Services

Comprehensive Manufacturing Services

We provide a full spectrum of precision engineering and manufacturing services tailored for the tyre industry and beyond.

Precision Machining

High-accuracy machining of complex industrial parts with tight tolerances. Specializing in tyre building drums, clamping rings, BPR, bead rings, PCI rings and solid rims using advanced VMC and CNC equipment.

CNC Turning

Heavy-duty CNC turning operations with OD up to 800mm and length 1000mm. Capable of handling large-diameter workpieces with precise dimensional accuracy for all industrial component requirements.

Fabrication Works

Complete fabrication including structural assemblies, weld fixtures, jigs and custom industrial equipment. From raw material to finished fabricated assembly, we handle the entire process.

Drilling & Cutting

Hand drilling machines (4 Nos., 4", 5", 7"), cut-off saw (350mm), radial drilling machine (GSP Make), hand profile cutter, and semi-automatic Pog cutting for complete cutting solutions.

Welding Services

Professional ARC welding services with 2 dedicated arc welders operating ARC-400 machines. Providing robust, quality weld joints for structural, pressure, and precision weld requirements.

Milling Operations

Surface and profile milling using Alcera Make milling machines. Capable of flat surface generation, slot milling, contouring and step milling for complex component geometries.

Tyre Building Drum Manufacturing

Specialized manufacturing of tyre building drums — the core equipment component used in tyre manufacturing. Precision-engineered to exact customer drawings and standards.

Design & Development

In-house design capability using AutoCAD, SolidWorks and Mastercam. Our 3-member design team can develop components from concept drawings to production-ready machining programs.

Quality Inspection

Comprehensive inspection using Vernier calipers, micrometers, dial gauges, height gauges and slip gauges. Every part is measured and verified to meet customer-specified tolerances.

Dial Gauge

Height Gauge
